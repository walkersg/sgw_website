About Me

I am a data-oriented behavior-science researcher and clinician. I have experience in orgainzational behavior, business intelligence, process improvement, and clinic development. I have recently taken up the hobby of Artificial Intelligence and software development, which I will showcase as I continue to develop my skills.

My specific interests include:
